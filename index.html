Virtual Reality

VR transports the user into the world you want her to experience. The immersion in VR is 100%- she will believe she is flying in a plane even though in reality she is seated on a chair in a office cubicle.
A few years back it would have been hard to believe, if told that a potential client based in London could walk through a factory in India, that too without leaving his home.
It's possible in VR. Similarly, A live music lover from the US can not only join a music show in VR,he can also interact with other music lovers from different countries to enjoy a live show.
An automobile manufacturing company in Japan can train new engineers in India. They can do this repeatedly using the same VR software solution, in multiple locations at lower costs than before.
A cruise ship can give their patrons a virtual tour of their ship in VR. A tennis or cricket professional doesn't have to miss practice & can get coached even if she/he can't leave their homes.
The list is long & numerous businesses & organisations are taking advantage of VR to reach clients, decrease costs & improve sales.
It is particularly advantageous for exhibiting large scale projects, facilities, machines or processes . You can also capture the screen in VR to convert it into a video.
VR solutions are being used for Onboarding, Training, Design, Product Marketing, Branding, Customer Engagement, Events, Pop-Up Stores, Operations, Maintenance & delivering Lectures.
VR is mobile, you can deploy it in an expo / exhibition or a client's facility, at home or in your workplace.
Some of the most challenging, time consuming & costly business problems can now be easily solved with Virtual Reality based software & hardware solutions.
You can refer to few of the case studies listed below to learn how your business can use VR.

Augumented Reality

If VR is about Immersion, AR is about adding a dash of digital magic to your everyday life. The term Augment means the real world or surface is augmented by the unreal or digital.
AR is more consumer oriented & easy to grasp. Millions of mobile users on Snapchat & Facebook are applying AR filters to everyday photos & videos. These platforms have created specialised software so that users can create custom AR filters.
But AR is not only about the light hearted fun of social media filters, it's being used for more serious stuff like Product Demos, Experiential Marketing Campaigns, Games, Training, Print , Education, Data Visualization etc.
The biggest advantage of AR over VR is that it can be deployed with any smartphone, without the need for expensive head gears.The development process is faster & large no of viewers can be engaged at a low cost.
