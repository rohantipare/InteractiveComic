FridayFive - BEST of the week gone by in AR/VR/MR/3D/AI & Games - 18

Mar 21, 2020

Latest update on the #FurTool that we are developing for the #unityasset store. I have posted earlier iterations twice before. With this version we are nearing the finish line & then we go to testing with 3D artists. This was the drill last time too.

The artist who is helping out with the test, is an experienced 3d hand with good #texturing skills. Up till now its the #programmer :-) who was painting the textures in Gimp. We also hope to test with non-BEAR animals & human models too.

Shoutout to 3d character artists, if you would like to contribute character model for the test, we would be happy to use it & share the results with that artist.

NOTE- The model will still be yours & will not be added to our #showreel. We will share the results & you can decide if it can be displayed or not.

Appreciate it if some of the #3d #characters #artists who struggle with creating 3d-Hair or Fur in their work, could throw in their comments or a word of advise.
