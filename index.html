Virtual Reality

A few years back it would have been hard to believe, if told that a potential client based in London could walk through a factory in India, that too without leaving his home. This is now made possible by virtual reality experiences. Similarly, VR based solutions are being used for Onboarding, Training, Design, Product Marketing, Branding, Customer Engagement, Events, Pop-Up Stores, Operations, Maintenance & delivering Lectures.

Augmented Reality

AR is more consumer oriented & easy to grasp. Millions of mobile users on Snapchat & Facebook are applying AR filters to everyday photos & videos. But AR is not limited to social media filters, it has become a darling of advertisers & marketers for Product Promotions, Marketing Campaigns, Training, Education, Data Visualization etc. The biggest advantage of AR over VR is that it can be deployed with any smartphone, without the need for expensive head gears.The development process is faster & large no of viewers can be engaged at a low cost. A large number of businesses & organisations are taking advantage of AR/ VR / 3D to reach clients, decrease costs & improve sales.
